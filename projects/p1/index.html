How we started?

AbbVie is a pharmaceutical company that discovers, develops and markets both biopharmaceuticals and small molecule drugs. It originated in 2003 as a spin-off of Abbott Laboratories.

What we do?

We combine advanced science with expertise to make strides in drug and treatment discovery, making a remarkable impact on people's lives.

How do you fit in

We are scientists, engineers, researchers, communicators, manufacturing specialists, regulatory experts, marketing professionals and more. We’re also parents, brothers, sisters, friends, community leaders and volunteers. We come together every day to create, discover and deliver new ways to improve people’s health.
